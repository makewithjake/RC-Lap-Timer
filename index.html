Built for the Track

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

Camera-Based Detection

No hardware needed. Uses your phone's camera as a motion-sensing finish line. Works with any RC car.

Custom Trigger Zone

Draw your detection line directly on the live feed. Only pixels in the zone are processed — maximum battery life.

Real-Time Lap Audio

TTS voice announces every lap time the moment your car crosses. Eyes stay on the track.

Offline Ready

Full airplane-mode support via service worker. Works at remote tracks with zero signal.

Glove-Friendly Controls

Extra-large buttons and high-contrast OLED display built for use at arm's length.

Session History

Every session saved locally. Review lap tables, charts, and personal bests anytime.

Simple by Design

Three Steps to Your First Lap

01

Point & Draw

Open the app, aim the camera at your finish line, drag to draw your Trigger Zone on the live feed.

02

Lock & Calibrate

Freeze exposure, test sensitivity with the Virtual LED indicator, set your debounce window.

03

Race

Hit Start. The timer runs automatically every time your car crosses the Trigger Zone.

Free. Instant. No App Store.

RC Timer is a Progressive Web App. Install it directly from your browser — no app store, no account, no cost.

Already using a browser? Open the app →

iOS Safari
  1. Open this page in Safari on your iPhone or iPad
  2. Tap the Share button ⬆
  3. Scroll down and tap “Add to Home Screen”
  4. Tap “Add” to confirm
Android Chrome
  1. Open this page in Chrome on Android
  2. Tap the three-dot menu ⋮
  3. Tap “Add to Home Screen” or “Install App”
  4. Tap “Install” to confirm
Desktop Chrome / Edge
  1. Look for the install icon in the address bar (computer icon)
  2. Click it and select “Install”
  3. Or open the menu → “Install RC Timer”
